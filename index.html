MISSION DRUG
FREE INDIA



“Drug Free India” & “ Save Youth” mission under Himalayan Stallion promotes Natural Fitness Practices and creates awareness on drugs & steroids abuse in the industry. Himalayan Stallion encourages the youth to spread further awareness on Natural Fitness Practices. Today, Youth need the right path to build their career in the Sports & Fitness Industry. Jeet Selal initiated “Drug Free India” & “Save Youth” mission to create more awareness on drugs abuse in the industry and aware the youth of its life threatening consequences that could be passed to the coming generation. Drug Free India and Save Youth mission are the pioneers to start "Himalayan Stallion Academy" and "Himalayan Stallion Sports Festival" by the guidance of Jeet Selal.
